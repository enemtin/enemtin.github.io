About Emma Nemtin

As a quintessential early adopter, I am constantly exploring how emerging technologies can connect people and solve problems.

I have a strong love for growth hacker marketing and a critical eye for creative branding. I believe marketing has long been an art and now it’s starting to be a science. I love chatting with other creative marketers and in order to merge my passion for marketing and technology I have dived head first into web development where I have become well versed in HTML5 and CSS3.

In my spare time I'm a workshop leader and mentor for Girls Learning Code, a non profit that puts on technology camps and workshops for young girls who want to use technology to change the world.
